Renewal FAQs

Know what to expect.

What is included in a landscape renewal?

The scope may include cleanup, pruning, plant removal or replacement, bed reshaping, edging, mulch, and targeted lawn improvements.

Can you work with my existing plants?

Yes. We preserve healthy plants that fit the plan and focus investment where changes will have the most value.

Is landscape renewal less expensive than a full redesign?

It often can be because useful existing features remain. The final cost depends on property size, overgrowth, materials, and the amount of new installation.
