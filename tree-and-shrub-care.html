Tree and shrub FAQs

Answers before we trim.

When should shrubs be trimmed in Knoxville?

Timing varies by plant species and whether it flowers on old or new growth. We consider the plant and season before recommending work.

Do you remove tree and shrub debris?

Yes. Cleanup and removal of normal trimming debris are included in the agreed project scope.

Do you remove large hazardous trees?

Our services focus on landscape trees, shrubs, trimming, and suitable removals. Large or hazardous trees may require a specialized tree service; we will be direct if a project falls outside our scope.
