Lawn and bed FAQs

Helpful answers.

When is the best time to install landscape beds in Knoxville?

Spring and fall are excellent planting seasons, though many bed, mulch, and cleanup projects can be completed throughout the growing season.

Can you refresh existing beds without redesigning everything?

Yes. We can redefine edges, remove overgrowth, refresh mulch, and selectively replace plants while preserving what works.

Do you help select plants?

Yes. We recommend plants based on sunlight, drainage, mature size, maintenance preferences, and the style of your home.
